About this Project

Social Media sentiments represent some people's deepest feelings. Some individuals with suicidal tendencies share posts and comments in Social Media and these could be digital clues. Our tool aims to assist and identify such tendencies and help prevent.

Team members are Allen, Jenny, Mehreen, and Rommel.

Methodology

Using Reddit posts and python-crfSuite....we used Machine Learning and NLP to perform analysis on Social Media posts.
